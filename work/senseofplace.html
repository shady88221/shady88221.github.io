Sense of the Place

These Posters are design for Oakleigh Angelician Church. The breif indicates we need to design a sense of a place. In order to visualised the sense, the poster has been designed as two very long and narrow rectangle shape, because it will be stick on the wall and the position will be a bit higher than audience's shoulder. Therefore, audience should be rising their head to look the poster, which creates a feeling of awe. What's more, the color palette of the poster symbolises different time of the church, such as day and night, warm and cold. The minimalism font and illustration are also symbolising the purity of the church.
