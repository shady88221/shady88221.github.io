Festival Poster

The brief requests to design a poster for the upcoming festival in Melbourne, Australia. The StereoSonic is one of the biggest rave music fastival in Meblourne and the tech-music is the main stream of this wonderful festival. Therefore, in order to express the sense of technology and the Si-fi of this festival, we decide to use old-fashioned Neon as our theme of the poster and combined with a robotic Dj table and a vintage robot illustration on a A1 size black brick wall.
