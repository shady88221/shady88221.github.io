Rebranding Project for CRED (formerly Libra Credit)

The client wanted me to help them with a rebranding project, because their older company name has some legal problems in America. (According to American law, libra is not a word that can be registed as a company name). Also they were a small firm before and it was not a problem. However, now they are so big and ready to be listed on NASDAQ. The challenge in this project is they want something formal, reliable but also very refresh(Smell like teen spirit..lool). Therefore, through loads of research and experiment, we provide a solution that not only can be visually related to old-fashioned business design but also could make you feel super young and fresh.
FYI, This project is too big to put everything on this webpage so I have splited it into 2 parts. This part mostly is about early research and identity, such as name, logo, color, applicaitons and so on.
