Fanzine

In this breif, we need to design a fanzine of a subculture. After reseached most contemporary subcultures, the vintage pixelated gaming culture has became the theme of the zine. Many pixelated characters and gaming strategy are included in this zine. Without any doubt, the 8 bit Nintendo entertainment system are the major element throughout the zine.
