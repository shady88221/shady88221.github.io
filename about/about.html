WHAT CAN I DO

Brand identity, I have extensive experience in working on large-scale brand identity projects. This means fluency in moving from understanding and interpreting strategy to developing initial concepts and creating brand guidelines to guiding implementation across a range of different applications for digital, space and print.

Editorial design, I understand what’s needed to effectively and compellingly bring content to life having worked on books, manuals, magazines and printed publications.

Information graphics, It will be making sense of complex org charts, business flows and data and ability to present information in an easy to understand and memorable way.

Typography, I have a love and obsession for the finer details of type and knowledge of how to express emotions and evoke reactions through type.

Packaging Design, I got excellent understanding of form, structure, and materials, and ability to articulate clear information hierarchy and great design on anything from food products to electronic goods.

Exhibition graphics & signage, I can communicating in space to create clear and immersive visual journeys for retail spaces, events or exhibitions.

Production knowledge, I have solid knowledge in production and artworking to liaise with suppliers and vendors in producing exquisitely executed pieces of print design.

Languages, I also am a bilingual designers with fluency in written and spoken English and Mandarin.
